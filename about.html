About FlowFit Studio

FlowFit Studio brings together functional training, mobility and mindful movement. Our mission is to help people move better, feel stronger, and enjoy sustainable fitness.

Our Approach

We combine strength, mobility and breath work to build resilient bodies. Programs scale for all levels, focusing on technique, posture and gradual progression.

Meet Our Manager

Alex Morgan founded FlowFit Studio in 2018 with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where anyone — from complete beginners to seasoned athletes — can feel confident moving, training and growing stronger.

With over a decade of experience in functional training, Pilates, and mobility coaching, Alex believes fitness should be sustainable, enjoyable, and deeply connected to daily life.
