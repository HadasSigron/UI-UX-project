Frequently Asked Questions

Classes, levels, bookings and studio policies — all in one place. Use the search below.

FAQ

New here? Start with the Beginner questions below.

Class levels

We offer Level 1 (Beginner), Level 2 (Mixed), and Level 3 (Advanced). Not sure? Book a short intro call and we’ll recommend the best fit.

First visit

Yes — your first class is free this week. Arrive 10 minutes early and bring water.

Booking

Book online via the schedule. Cancellations are free up to 6 hours before the class. Late cancellation or no-show may be charged as a class.

Waitlist

If a spot opens, you’ll receive an email/SMS and be auto-added. If you can’t make it, please remove yourself to free the spot for others.

Membership

Yes: monthly unlimited memberships and 5/10/20 class packs (valid 6 months). Students get 10% off with ID.

Prices

Prices are listed on the pricing page. Seasonal promotions appear on our Instagram and newsletter.

Personal training

Absolutely. We start with an assessment, set goals, and build a plan tailored to your schedule and preferences.

Beginner

Start with Level 1 and Foundations workshops. After 4–6 sessions, try a Level 2 class to progress safely.

What to bring

Comfortable clothes, clean training shoes, and water. We provide mats and basic equipment.

Injuries

Please consult your doctor first. Let the coach know before class; we’ll offer safe modifications.

Pregnancy

With medical approval, yes. We’ll adapt intensity and movements. Consider our specialized prenatal sessions.

Kids & Teens

We run occasional workshops for ages 12–16. Check our schedule or contact us for upcoming dates.

Seniors

Yes. Join Level 1 and Mobility & Balance sessions — designed to be joint-friendly and safe.

Facilities

Yes — showers, lockers, and filtered water are available. Bring your own towel.

Parking

Street parking after 18:00 is free. We’re a 5-minute walk from the Central station (bus lines 4/18/42).

Refunds

Class packs are non-refundable once activated. Memberships can be paused for travel or medical reasons.

Gift cards

Yes — digital gift cards for any amount or class pack. Valid for 12 months.

Corporate

We love team sessions! Contact us for on-site or in-studio programs tailored to your company.

Workshops

Yes — mobility, strength foundations, and special guest coaches. Check the media page for photos.

Online

From time to time, yes — follow our newsletter to get the next online cycle dates.

App & notifications

Use our web app to book; enable email/SMS reminders and waitlist alerts in your profile settings.

Languages

Classes are in English; some coaches also speak Hebrew and Russian — ask at the front desk.
